Commentary: Provide additional advice to man-mode

11.109.1 emacspeak-man Commands

Interactive Command: emacspeak-man-browse-man-page nil

Browse the man page –read it a paragraph at a time

Interactive Command: emacspeak-man-speak-this-section nil

Speak current section
